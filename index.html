Who am I

Christopher Graham is a 17-year-old teenager who enjoys donating blood, binge-watching boxed sets and walking. He is Kind and Creative, but can also be very lazy and a bit dull.

He is New Zealand who defines himself as bisexual. He is currently at school. He is allergic to wasps. He has a severe phobia of balloons

Physically, Christopher is in pretty good shape. He is tall with pale skin, red hair and blue eyes.

He lives in a working class neighbourhood. His parents separated when he was small, but remained friends and provided a happy, stable home.

Christopher goes to Shelbyville School, where his favourite subjects are P.E., biology and sports science. He loves his teacher Mr Thomson but hates Miss Parker whose interests include putting children in detention for no reason. He is currently in a relationship with Elysia Shauna Black. Elysia is the same age as him and works as a student.

Christopher's best friend is a teenager called Jay Crawford. They have a very fiery friendship. He also hangs around with Wilma Jones and Ernest Burns. They enjoy baking together.
